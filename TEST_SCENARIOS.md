
# Bob & Alice Test Scenarios

Here are some example "scripts" to read when testing the voice agent.

## Scenario 1: Kitchen Renovation (Bob -> Alice)
_Start with Bob (Default)_

**You**: "Hi Bob, I'm thinking about renovating my kitchen."
**Bob**: _(Asks about layout/budget)_
**You**: "It's about 200 square feet. I want a modern look with an island."
**Bob**: _(Discusses materials)_
**You**: "I'm not sure about the colors. Can I talk to Alice about the design?"
**Bob**: _(Transfers to Alice)_
**Alice**: _(Greets you)_
**You**: "Hi Alice, I was thinking about white cabinets with a dark countertop."

## Scenario 2: Bathroom Remodel
**You**: "I want to redo my master bathroom."
**Bob**: _(Asks about fixtures)_
**You**: "We need a double vanity and a walk-in shower. The current one is too small."
**Bob**: _(Asks about budget)_
**You**: "We have about $15,000 for the project."

## Scenario 3: Quick Transfer
**You**: "Transfer me to Alice right away, I have a design question."
**Bob**: _(Transfers)_
**Alice**: "Hi there! I'm Alice, the design specialist. What can I help you with?"
**You**: "What are the trending colors for living rooms this year?"

## Scenario 4: Error Handling
**You**: "Sdhf skdjfh skdjfh" _(Mumous/Quiet)_
**Bob**: "I didn't quite catch that. Could you repeat it?"
